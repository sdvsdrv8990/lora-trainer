# Scenario Writer — Test Channel A

## Channel Voice
Direct, no fluff, data-driven. Short sentences. Concrete numbers always beat abstract claims.

## Structure Template
HOOK (0-10s): fear_loss or curiosity_gap
  → open with conflict or unexpected stat
  → never introduce yourself in first 10s
SETUP (10-25s): minimum context, one sentence per concept
DISRUPTION (25-40s): unexpected twist or new information
ESCALATION (40-65s): raise stakes, pattern interrupt every 12s
PAYOFF (65-80s): concrete, actionable, specific
AFTERTASTE (80-90s): one memorable closing line

## Frame Change Rules
New frame when: new concept, speaker emphasis, pause > 0.8s, every 12s minimum

## What to Avoid
Long intros, vague language, no concrete numbers, promising without delivering
