# Hook Engine — Wallet from the EU

## Core Hook Formula
HOOK = promise of reward + proof it's worth watching + minimum cognitive entry cost
First sentence must create a question the viewer cannot leave unanswered.

## Preferred Hook Types (ranked by channel fit)

**1. Stat Shock + Curiosity Gap**
Open with a number that breaks the viewer's mental model.
"87% of day traders lose money. Not because they're bad at math. Because the game is designed that way."

**2. Fear Loss**
Something the viewer has, is at risk. Make it personal.
"Every time you get paid, a system quietly takes a cut you never approved."

**3. Social Violation**
Break a widely accepted belief immediately.
"Saving money is actually making you poorer. Here's the math."

**4. Paradox Hook**
State something that sounds impossible but is true.
"The dumbest investors consistently outperform the smartest ones. And it's not an accident."

## Hook Duration
Target: 8-12 seconds of narration
Rule: by second 10, viewer must have a question they need answered to leave

## What Never Works as a Hook
- Introducing yourself
- "Today we're going to talk about..."
- Slow context-building before the conflict
- Asking a question without implying you have the answer

## Reset Points
Place every 12-15 seconds throughout the video:
- New visual element appears
- Punchline lands (pause after)
- Number revealed
- Unexpected turn in narrative

## Ending Hook (comment bait)
Always end with a binary question — two clear camps:
"So which is it — is [X] a broken system or is [Y] the real problem? Drop your take below."
