# Image Prompts — Wallet from the EU

## Required Prefix (add to every prompt)
"flat 2d animation, white background, bold black outlines, simple round stickman characters, clean vector style"

## Required Negative (add to every prompt)
"realistic, photo, 3d render, complex background, text, watermark, detailed faces, gradient"

## Character Color System (LOCKED — never change)
- Yellow stickman = average worker / viewer avatar
- Blue stickman = narrator / expert / bank official
- Green stickman = wealthy person / investor (positive outcome)
- Red stickman = villain / scammer / bad actor
- Purple stickman with flower = rich woman / luxury consumer
- Orange stickman = government official / regulator
- Gray stickman = crowd / generic mass of people

## Character Construction
Each character = round head + oval body + simple arms/legs
Emotions shown through: head tilt, arm position, sweat drops, question marks above head
Eyes = two small black dots only
No mouths unless expression requires it

## Object Style Rules
Money = green rectangle bills OR gold coin circles
Buildings = simple box shapes with minimal windows
Charts/graphs = bold lines, two colors max
Arrows = thick, clear direction
Icons = flat, single color, no shadows

## Scene Composition Rules
- Max 4 elements per frame
- Text labels allowed: bold, black, large font, positioned above/below object
- White space is your friend — do not crowd the frame
- One focal point per frame

## Prompt Formula
[scene description] + [character positions and colors] + [key objects] + [emotional tone] + [prefix]

## Examples by Scene Type

**Character explaining concept:**
"yellow stickman standing left, blue stickman standing right pointing at large red arrow going down, simple bar chart in background, confused expression, flat 2d animation, white background, bold black outlines, simple round stickman characters"

**Money flowing / transaction:**
"green dollar bills flying from yellow stickman toward large gray building labeled BANK, yellow stickman looking worried, flat 2d animation, white background, bold black outlines"

**Comparison / versus:**
"two columns: left side yellow stickman with small coin stack, right side green stickman with tall coin tower, bold dividing line in center, flat 2d animation, white background, bold black outlines"

**Chart / data moment:**
"large red declining line chart taking up 70% of frame, small yellow stickman in bottom right looking up at chart with sweat drops, flat 2d animation, white background, bold black outlines"
